WHAT DO WE OFFER AT DEDOVIĆGarage

DEDOVIĆGarage is an independent service center owned and operated by local experts, authorized for mechanical repairs and located in Sarajevo. At DEDOVIĆGarage, we can take care of all your automotive needs, including servicing new and older model vehicles, light commercial vehicles, and SUVs. We offer mechanical repair services such as diagnostic scanning, brake and clutch repairs, steering and suspension systems, exhaust systems, transmission, final drive, and electrical repairs. We also provide vehicle pre-purchase inspections.

In addition to the aforementioned services, DEDOVIĆGarage offers top-notch car detailing services, where we pay special attention to the thorough cleaning, restoration, and finishing of your vehicle, ensuring both the interior and exterior look impeccable. Our team uses only the highest quality products and techniques to make your vehicle shine.

For those who are enthusiasts of enhanced performance, DEDOVIĆGarage also provides chip tuning services, optimizing your vehicle's software to improve engine performance, including increased power and torque, as well as potentially improved fuel efficiency. Our experts use advanced tuning technology, ensuring that modifications are carried out safely and effectively, adhering to all manufacturer standards and specifications.

Our team of experts is committed to providing high-quality services using the most modern technologies and tools, ensuring that your vehicle is in the best possible condition. Trust us with your vehicle and allow it to receive the best possible care and enhancement at DEDOVIĆGarage.

HAMZA DEDOVIĆ

Computer Specialist / Cleaner

HAMZA DEDOVIĆ is an expert in VCDS coding and chip tuning at DEDOVIĆGarage. As a VCDS coding specialist, Hamza deals with accessing the electronic systems of vehicles, especially for Volkswagen Group vehicles, to customize and adjust functions according to the owner's preferences. This may include activating hidden or additional functional elements in the vehicle, providing personalization and performance enhancement.

In addition, Hamza is an expert in chip tuning, which is the process of optimizing the software program of the engine control unit (ECU) to improve vehicle performance. By adjusting parameters such as fuel delivery, ignition, and turbocharger pressure, it's possible to achieve increased engine power and torque, as well as improved throttle response.

Hamza also works alongside Amra Kozo, a car detailing specialist at DEDOVIĆGarage. As a car detailing expert, Hamza ensures that every vehicle that passes through their hands looks impeccably clean and shiny. Using high-quality products and techniques, they together ensure that the interior and exterior of the vehicle look like new.

With his multiple skills and expertise, Hamza DEDOVIĆ is a key member of the team at DEDOVIĆGarage, providing high-quality VCDS coding, chip tuning, and car detailing services.
